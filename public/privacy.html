Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Akaal Autohub ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information, in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us, request a quote, or book a service, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Vehicle information (make, model, year, service needed)
  • Messages and details you provide through our website, text/SMS, phone, or in-person at our shop
  • Appointment and service history
  • Payment information processed securely through our payment provider (we do not store full card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Schedule, confirm, and manage your appointments
  • Provide quotes, service updates, and estimated completion times
  • Send appointment reminders and follow-up communications
  • Respond to inquiries submitted by phone, text, or our website
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Meet our legal, accounting, and tax obligations

3. SMS / Text Messaging Consent

By providing your phone number through our website, booking form, or in-person, you consent to receive text messages (SMS) from Akaal Autohub related to appointment scheduling, confirmations, reminders, quotes, and service updates.

  • Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us.
  • Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message. Reply HELP for assistance.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or mobile opt-in information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your number is used solely to communicate with you about your service with Akaal Autohub.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted third-party platforms to operate our business and communicate with customers, including:

  • GoHighLevel (GHL) — our customer relationship management and messaging platform, used to send and receive texts, emails, and manage appointment scheduling.
  • AppointWise — an AI-assisted booking tool that may respond to initial customer inquiries and help schedule appointments.
  • Payment processors, used solely to process transactions securely.

These providers process information on our behalf and are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we've authorized. They do not use your information for their own marketing purposes.

5. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Access to customer information is limited to staff who need it to perform their job duties.

6. Data Retention

We retain customer information for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain accurate business and tax records, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your information at any time, subject to our legal and recordkeeping requirements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent to receive marketing communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.
